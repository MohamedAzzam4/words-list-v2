# Unit 3: Familie — German Phrases

## 1. Core Everyday Phrases
### P01
**German:** Das ist meine Familie.
**Meaning:** This is my family.
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** die Familie, mein
**Note:** Simple introduction when showing a family photo.

### P02
**German:** Meine Mutter heißt Petra.
**Meaning:** My mother is called Petra.
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** die Mutter, mein
**Note:** Introducing a family member by name.

## 5. Informal / Slang Phrases
### S01
**German:** Echt? Dein Bruder ist so groß?
**Meaning:** Really? Your brother is that tall?
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** dein, der Bruder, groß
**Note:** "Echt?" expresses surprise — very common in spoken German.

### S02
**German:** Mega süß, das Baby!
**Meaning:** Super cute, the baby!
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** das Baby
**Note:** "Mega" is informal for "super/really."

### S03
**German:** Na ja, meine Familie ist klein.
**Meaning:** Well, my family is small.
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** mein, die Familie, klein
**Note:** "Na ja" = casual "well..." filler.

### S04
**German:** Keine Ahnung, wie alt sein Cousin ist.
**Meaning:** No idea how old his cousin is.
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** alt, sein, der Cousin
**Note:** "Keine Ahnung" = "no idea."

### S05
**German:** Voll cool! Deine Schwester spielt Gitarre?
**Meaning:** Totally cool! Your sister plays guitar?
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** dein, die Schwester, spielen
**Note:** "Voll cool" is casual youth slang.

### S06
**German:** Alles klar, dein Onkel lebt in Spanien.
**Meaning:** Alright, your uncle lives in Spain.
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** dein, der Onkel, leben
**Note:** "Alles klar" = "okay/got it."

### S07
**German:** Passt schon, meine Großeltern kommen morgen.
**Meaning:** It's fine, my grandparents are coming tomorrow.
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** mein, die Großeltern
**Note:** "Passt schon" = "no worries."

### S08
**German:** Echt, dein Sohn ist so klein!
**Meaning:** Wow, your son is so small!
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** dein, der Sohn, klein
**Note:** Expressing surprise at a child's size.
