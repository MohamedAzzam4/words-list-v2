# Unit 22: Kommunikation — German Phrases

## 1. Core Everyday Phrases
### P01
**German:** Ich ziehe meine Jacke an.
**Meaning:** I'm putting on my jacket.
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** anziehen, die Jacke
**Note:** Separable verb "anziehen" — prefix goes to the end.

### P02
**German:** Zieh bitte die Schuhe aus!
**Meaning:** Please take off the shoes!
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** ausziehen, der Schuh
**Note:** Common request when entering a German home.

### P03
**German:** Er trägt ein blaues Hemd.
**Meaning:** He is wearing a blue shirt.
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** tragen, blau, das Hemd
**Note:** "Tragen" means both to wear and to carry.

### P04
**German:** Das steht dir gut!
**Meaning:** That looks good on you!
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** Das steht dir gut!, stehen
**Note:** Fixed compliment phrase — very commonly used.

### P05
**German:** Kann ich das anprobieren?
**Meaning:** Can I try this on?
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** anprobieren
**Note:** Essential phrase for clothing shopping.

### P06
**German:** Welche Größe haben Sie?
**Meaning:** What size do you have?
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** die Größe
**Note:** Formal question from shop assistants.

### P07
**German:** Die Hose ist zu eng.

**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral

## 5. Informal / Slang Phrases
### S01
**German:** Mega! Das steht dir voll gut!
**Meaning:** Awesome! That really looks great on you!
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** stehen
**Note:** "Mega" and "voll" are casual intensifiers popular with young speakers.

### S02
**German:** Echt schöne Jacke!
**Meaning:** Really nice jacket!
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** die Jacke
**Note:** "Echt" as a casual way to say "really."

### S03
**German:** Keine Ahnung, welche Größe ich brauche.
**Meaning:** No idea what size I need.
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** die Größe
**Note:** "Keine Ahnung" = casual "I don't know."

### S04
**German:** Na ja, die Farbe ist okay.
**Meaning:** Well, the color is okay.
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** die Farbe
**Note:** "Na ja" = lukewarm agreement.

### S05
**German:** Alles klar, ich ziehe mich an.
**Meaning:** All right, I'll get dressed.
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** anziehen
**Note:** "Alles klar" = casual acknowledgment.

### S06
**German:** Passt schon, die Hose ist voll bequem.
**Meaning:** It's fine, the pants are really comfortable.
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** die Hose, passen
**Note:** "Passt schon" = casual "it's okay." "Voll" intensifies.
