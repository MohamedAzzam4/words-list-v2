# Unit 4: Wohnen — German Phrases

## 1. Core Everyday Phrases
### P01
**German:** Wie viel kostet das?
**Meaning:** How much does that cost?
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** Wie viel kostet das?, kosten
**Note:** The most essential shopping phrase.

### P02
**German:** Das kostet zehn Euro.
**Meaning:** That costs ten euros.
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** kosten, der Euro
**Note:** Standard answer to a price question.

## 5. Informal / Slang Phrases
### S01
**German:** Echt? Das ist so billig!
**Meaning:** Really? That is so cheap!
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** billig
**Note:** "Echt?" expresses surprise.

### S02
**German:** Mega schön, die Lampe!
**Meaning:** Super beautiful, the lamp!
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** die Lampe, schön
**Note:** "Mega" is youthful slang for "really/super."

### S03
**German:** Na ja, der Preis ist okay.
**Meaning:** Well, the price is okay.
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** der Preis
**Note:** "Na ja" = casual "well..." filler.

### S04
**German:** Keine Ahnung, wo der Supermarkt ist.
**Meaning:** No idea where the supermarket is.
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** der Supermarkt
**Note:** "Keine Ahnung" = "no clue."

### S05
**German:** Voll teuer! Das kaufe ich nicht.
**Meaning:** Totally expensive! I'm not buying that.
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** teuer, kaufen
**Note:** "Voll" = informal "totally/really."

### S06
**German:** Passt schon, ich bezahle das.
**Meaning:** It's fine, I'll pay for that.
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** bezahlen
**Note:** "Passt schon" = "no worries / it's fine."

### S07
**German:** Alles klar, wir brauchen den Tisch.
**Meaning:** Alright, we need the table.
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** brauchen, der Tisch
**Note:** "Alles klar" = "okay/got it."

### S08
**German:** Echt hässlich, der Schrank!
**Meaning:** Really ugly, the wardrobe!
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** hässlich, der Schrank
**Note:** "Echt" intensifies the adjective informally.
