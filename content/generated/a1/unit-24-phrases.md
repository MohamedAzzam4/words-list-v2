# Unit 24: Review & Practice — German Phrases

## 1. Core Everyday Phrases
### P01
**German:** Wir feiern heute ein Fest.
**Meaning:** We are celebrating a festival today.
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** feiern, das Fest
**Note:** General statement about a celebration.

### P02
**German:** Frohe Weihnachten!
**Meaning:** Merry Christmas!
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** Weihnachten
**Note:** The most important Christmas greeting.

### P03

**Register:** neutral

## 5. Informal / Slang Phrases
### S01
**German:** Mega Feuerwerk gestern!
**Meaning:** Awesome fireworks yesterday!
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** das Feuerwerk
**Note:** "Mega" = casual intensifier for excitement.

### S02
**German:** Echt? Du feierst Karneval nicht?
**Meaning:** Really? You don't celebrate Carnival?
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** feiern, der Karneval
**Note:** "Echt?" = informal surprise.

### S03
**German:** Na ja, Valentinstag ist nicht so mein Ding.
**Meaning:** Well, Valentine's Day isn't really my thing.
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** der Valentinstag
**Note:** "Na ja" = casual; "nicht so mein Ding" = informal "not my thing."

### S04
**German:** Voll schön, die Feier!
**Meaning:** Really beautiful, the celebration!
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** die Feier
**Note:** "Voll" = informal intensifier.

### S05
**German:** Passt schon, wir feiern morgen.
**Meaning:** It's fine, we'll celebrate tomorrow.
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** feiern
**Note:** "Passt schon" = casual "it's okay."

### S06
**German:** Keine Ahnung, was ich schenken soll.
**Meaning:** No idea what I should give as a gift.
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** schenken
**Note:** "Keine Ahnung" = casual "I don't know."
