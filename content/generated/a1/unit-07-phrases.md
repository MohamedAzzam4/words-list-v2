# Unit 7: Körper & Gesundheit — German Phrases

## 1. Core Everyday Phrases
### P01
**German:** Was ist dein Hobby?
**Meaning:** What is your hobby?
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** das Hobby
**Note:** Classic conversation starter for getting to know someone.

### P02
**German:** Ich höre gern Musik.
**Meaning:** I like listening to music.
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** hören, die Musik, gern
**Note:** "gern" after the verb means "like to do something."

### P03
**German:** Ich mache gern Sport.
**Meaning:** I like doing sports.
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** der Sport, gern
**Note:** "Sport machen" = to do sports (general phrase).

### P04
**German:** Der Film ist interessant.
**Meaning:** The film is interesting.
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** der Film, interessant
**Note:** A simple way to give your opinion on a movie.

### P05
**German:** Ich gehe gern ins Kino.
**Meaning:** I like going to the cinema.
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** das Kino, gern
**Note:** "ins" = in das. Very common leisure activity.

### P06
**German:** Das Konzert ist fantastisch!
**Meaning:** The concert is fantastic!
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** das Konzert, fantastisch
**Note:** Strong positive reaction.

### P07
**German:** Ich schwimme gern.
**Meaning:** I like swimming.
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** schwimmen, gern
**Note:** Swimming is one of the most popular hobbies in Germany.

### P08
**German:** Ich koche gern.
**Meaning:** I like cooking.
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** kochen, gern
**Note:** Cooking is a popular hobby for many Germans.

### P09
**German:** In meiner Freizeit lese ich.
**Meaning:** In my free time, I read.
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** die Freizeit
**Note:** Classic way to describe leisure activities.

### P10
**German:** Ich fahre gern Fahrrad.
**Meaning:** I like riding a bike.
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** das Fahrrad, gern
**Note:** "Fahrrad fahren" = to ride a bike.

### P11
**German:** Das Theater ist schön.
**Meaning:** The theater is nice.
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** das Theater
**Note:** Used for the building or the art form.

### P12
**German:** Das Museum ist interessant.
**Meaning:** The museum is interesting.
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** das Museum, interessant
**Note:** A common comment after a museum visit.

## 5. Informal / Slang Phrases
### S01
**German:** Das Kino ist echt toll!
**Meaning:** The cinema is really great!
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** das Kino, toll
**Note:** "echt" adds emphasis — very natural in spoken German.

### S02
**German:** Der Film ist mega langweilig!
**Meaning:** The movie is super boring!
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** der Film, langweilig
**Note:** "mega" = super/extremely — common youth slang.

### S03
**German:** Schwimmen ist voll cool!
**Meaning:** Swimming is totally cool!
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** schwimmen
**Note:** "voll" = totally/really in informal German.

### S04
**German:** Keine Ahnung, was mein Hobby ist.
**Meaning:** No idea what my hobby is.
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** das Hobby
**Note:** "Keine Ahnung" = no idea — extremely common.

### S05
**German:** Alles klar, wir gehen ins Konzert!
**Meaning:** All right, we're going to the concert!
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** das Konzert
**Note:** "Alles klar" = all right / got it.

### S06
**German:** Na ja, Tanzen ist nicht so mein Ding.
**Meaning:** Well, dancing is not really my thing.
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** tanzen
**Note:** "Na ja" = well; "nicht so mein Ding" = not my thing.

### S07
**German:** Das Konzert war echt fantastisch!
**Meaning:** The concert was really fantastic!
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** das Konzert, fantastisch
**Note:** Using "war" (was) for past events is acceptable at A1.

### S08
**German:** Mega, das Museum ist super!
**Meaning:** Awesome, the museum is great!
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** das Museum, super
**Note:** "mega" as an exclamation of enthusiasm.

### S09
**German:** Wandern ist echt super!
**Meaning:** Hiking is really great!
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** wandern, super
**Note:** Positive opinion about hiking with slang intensifier.

### S10
**German:** Ich koche voll gern!
**Meaning:** I totally love cooking!
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** kochen, gern
**Note:** "voll gern" = really like to — casual intensifier.
