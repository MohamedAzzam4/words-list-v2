# Unit 2: Zahlen & Farben — German Phrases

## 1. Core Everyday Phrases
### P01
**German:** Was ist Ihr Beruf?
**Meaning:** What is your profession?
**Level:** A1
**Register:** formal
**Used words:** was, der Beruf
**Note:** Standard formal question about someone's job.

### P02
**German:** Ich bin Lehrer.
**Meaning:** I am a teacher. (male)
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** der Lehrer
**Note:** In German, you omit the article when stating your profession.

### P03
**German:** Sie ist Lehrerin.
**Meaning:** She is a teacher. (female)
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** sie, die Lehrerin
**Note:** Female form of "Lehrer" — add "-in."

### P04
**German:** Er ist Arzt.
**Meaning:** He is a doctor.
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** er, der Arzt
**Note:** A common profession statement.

### P05
**German:** Ich bin Ärztin.
**Meaning:** I am a doctor. (female)
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** die Ärztin
**Note:** Female form of "Arzt."

### P06
**German:** Ich bin Student und lerne Deutsch.
**Meaning:** I am a student and I'm learning German.
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** der Student, lernen
**Note:** Combining profession with an activity.

### P07
**German:** Sie studiert in Berlin.
**Meaning:** She studies in Berlin.
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** sie, studieren
**Note:** "Studieren" means to study at a university.

### P08
**German:** Wo wohnen Sie?

**Level:** A1
**Register:** formal

### P09
**German:** Ich wohne in München.

**Register:** neutral

### P10
**German:** Woher kommen Sie?

**Register:** formal

### P11
**German:** Er arbeitet als Koch.
**Meaning:** He works as a cook.

**Register:** neutral

### P12

**Register:** neutral

### P13
**German:** Ich spreche Deutsch und Englisch.
**Meaning:** I speak German and English.
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** sprechen (spricht), die Sprache
**Note:** Stating your languages.

### P14

**Meaning:** He speaks three languages.
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** er, sprechen (spricht), die Sprache

## 5. Informal / Slang Phrases
### S01
**German:** Echt? Du bist Ärztin?
**Meaning:** Really? You're a doctor?
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** die Ärztin
**Note:** "Echt?" expresses surprise informally.

### S02
**German:** Mega! Du sprichst vier Sprachen?
**Meaning:** Awesome! You speak four languages?
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** sprechen (spricht), die Sprache
**Note:** "Mega" is youthful slang for "amazing."

### S03
**German:** Na ja, ich arbeite nicht so viel.
**Meaning:** Well, I don't work that much.
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** arbeiten
**Note:** "Na ja" = "well..." — a casual filler.

### S04
**German:** Keine Ahnung, wo er wohnt.
**Meaning:** No idea where he lives.
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** wo, er, wohnen
**Note:** "Keine Ahnung" = "no clue" — very common.

### S05
**German:** Alles klar, ich buchstabiere das.
**Meaning:** Alright, I'll spell that.
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** buchstabieren
**Note:** "Alles klar" = "okay/got it."

### S06
**German:** Passt schon, wir lernen das.
**Meaning:** It's fine, we'll learn that.
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** wir, lernen
**Note:** "Passt schon" = "no worries / it's fine."

### S07
**German:** Voll cool! Er ist Programmierer!
**Meaning:** Totally cool! He is a programmer!
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** er, der Programmierer
**Note:** "Voll cool" is casual youth slang.

### S08
**German:** Echt gut! Du studierst Medizin?
**Meaning:** Really good! You're studying medicine?
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** studieren
**Note:** Combining "echt" with a positive adjective.

### S09
**German:** Mega, du sprichst auch Spanisch!
**Meaning:** Awesome, you also speak Spanish!
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** sprechen (spricht)
**Note:** Expressing excitement about language skills.
