# Unit 20: Household & Kitchen — German Phrases

## 1. Core Everyday Phrases
### P01
**German:** Ich muss den Haushalt machen.
**Meaning:** I have to do the housework.
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** der Haushalt
**Note:** The most basic chore-related statement.

### P02
**German:** Die Waschmaschine läuft.
**Meaning:** The washing machine is running.
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** die Waschmaschine
**Note:** "Laufen" for machines that are in operation.

### P03
**German:** Der Kühlschrank ist leer.
**Meaning:** The fridge is empty.
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** der Kühlschrank
**Note:** Common household observation.

### P04
**German:** Der Herd ist noch an.
**Meaning:** The stove is still on.
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** der Herd
**Note:** Important safety-related phrase.

### P05

**Register:** neutral

### P06

### P24

## 5. Informal / Slang Phrases
### S01
**German:** Ich hab echt keine Lust aufzuräumen.
**Meaning:** I really don't feel like tidying up.
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** aufräumen
**Note:** "Echt keine Lust" — very common informal complaint.

### S02
**German:** Der Haushalt nervt mega!
**Meaning:** Housework is super annoying!
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** der Haushalt
**Note:** "Mega" intensifies the complaint.

### S03
**German:** Alles klar, ich spüle.
**Meaning:** All right, I'll do the dishes.
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** spülen
**Note:** "Alles klar" as casual agreement.

### S04
**German:** Na ja, der Kühlschrank ist halt leer.
**Meaning:** Well, the fridge is just empty.
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** der Kühlschrank
**Note:** "Na ja" + "halt" — casual, resigned tone.

### S05
**German:** Passt schon, ich mach den Müll.
**Meaning:** It's fine, I'll do the trash.
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** der Müll
**Note:** "Passt schon" — casual acceptance.

### S06
**German:** Keine Ahnung, wo die Gabel ist.
**Meaning:** No idea where the fork is.
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** die Gabel
**Note:** "Keine Ahnung" — common casual filler.

### S07
**German:** Voll die Unordnung hier!
**Meaning:** Such a mess here!
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** (general household context)
**Note:** "Voll die..." — colloquial emphasis.
