# Unit 23: Grammatik Basics — German Phrases

## 1. Core Everyday Phrases
### P01
**German:** Wie ist das Wetter heute?
**Meaning:** How is the weather today?
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** Wie ist das Wetter?, das Wetter
**Note:** The most essential weather question in German.

### P02
**German:** Es ist schönes Wetter.
**Meaning:** It's nice weather.
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** Es ist schönes Wetter., das Wetter
**Note:** Fixed expression used very frequently.

### P03
**German:** Die Sonne scheint.
**Meaning:** The sun is shining.
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** die Sonne, scheinen
**Note:** Classic weather description.

### P04
**German:** Es regnet heute.
**Meaning:** It's raining today.
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** regnen
**Note:** Very common weather statement.

### P05

**Meaning:** It's cold outside.
**Level:** A1
**Register:** neutral
**Used words:** kalt
**Note:** Everyday observation about temperature.

**Register:** neutral

## 5. Informal / Slang Phrases
### S01
**German:** Mega kalt heute!
**Meaning:** Super cold today!
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** kalt
**Note:** "Mega" is a casual intensifier, very popular among young speakers.

### S02
**German:** Echt? Es regnet schon wieder?
**Meaning:** Really? It's raining again?
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** regnen
**Note:** "Echt?" expresses informal surprise.

### S03
**German:** Na ja, das Wetter ist halt so.
**Meaning:** Well, the weather is just like that.
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** das Wetter
**Note:** "Na ja" and "halt" are casual filler words.

### S04
**German:** Voll sonnig, oder?
**Meaning:** Totally sunny, right?
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** sonnig
**Note:** "Voll" = informal intensifier.

### S05
**German:** Keine Ahnung, ob es heute schneit.
**Meaning:** No idea whether it snows today.
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** schneien
**Note:** "Keine Ahnung" = casual "I don't know."

### S06
**German:** Alles klar, ich nehme den Regenschirm.
**Meaning:** All right, I'll take the umbrella.
**Level:** A1/A2
**Register:** informal
**Used words:** (context: der Regen)
**Note:** "Alles klar" = casual acknowledgment. "Regenschirm" is compound of "Regen."
